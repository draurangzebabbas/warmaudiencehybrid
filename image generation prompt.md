Create a professional blog header illustration for an article titled: 

{Blog Post Title}

DIMENSIONS & FORMAT:
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (landscape)
- Resolution: 1792x1024 pixels
- Format: PNG, high quality

═══════════════════════════════════════
CRITICAL RULE — READ FIRST:
═══════════════════════════════════════
The central illustration MUST be directly and literally inspired by the article title.
Every image must look DIFFERENT from every other blog post image.
Achieve this by:
1. Drawing unique flat-style icons and illustrations relevant to the specific topic
2. Changing the dominant accent color per theme (see COLOR SYSTEM below)
3. Changing the central hero illustration per topic (see ILLUSTRATION LOGIC below)
DO NOT generate a generic "data nodes floating in space" image.
DO NOT repeat the same layout, composition, or icon set across articles.

═══════════════════════════════════════
ICON & ILLUSTRATION STYLE:
═══════════════════════════════════════

- Flat design with subtle depth shadows — NOT 3D, NOT photorealistic
- Icon style: rounded corners, bold strokes, 2–3 color fills maximum per icon
- All icons are ORIGINAL illustrations — do not reproduce any real brand logos
- Abstract references are fine: a blue "in" shape suggests LinkedIn without copying it
- Abstract envelope = email, abstract pipeline = CRM, abstract radar = tracker
- Icons should be large, bold, and crisp — not tiny decorative elements
- 3 to 5 distinct icons arranged around or supporting the hero illustration
- Icons cast soft colored drop shadows matching the accent color

═══════════════════════════════════════
TYPOGRAPHY:
═══════════════════════════════════════

- Render the article title as large bold white text inside the image
- Text: "{blog post title}"
- Font style: modern geometric sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar)
- Placement: left 50% of image, vertically centered
- Add a short glowing underline in the accent color beneath the first line of text
- Small label above the title in accent color, all caps, small size: "Warm Audience"
- Ensure all text is fully visible, unclipped, and legible at thumbnail size (400x225px)

═══════════════════════════════════════
COMPOSITION:
═══════════════════════════════════════

- Left half: title text block with label above and accent underline below
- Right half: the hero illustration + supporting icons arranged naturally
- Subtle background texture: very faint dot grid at 4% opacity
- Soft glow bloom behind the hero illustration in the accent color
- Slight vignette on all four edges
- No real human faces, no real photos, no stock imagery
- No watermarks, no URLs, no unrelated symbols

✓ At least 3 recognizable flat icons directly related to the topic are visible

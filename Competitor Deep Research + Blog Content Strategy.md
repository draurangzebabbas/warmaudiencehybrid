SECTION 1: Full Competitor Landscape
The LinkedIn lead generation space has 20+ active tools across four categories. Your app competes primarily in Category 1 and has a unique angle that no current tool fully captures.

Category A — Chrome Extension Scrapers
These are browser-dependent tools that require users to keep LinkedIn open. Direct but limited in scale.

1. Phantombuster
The market leader for LinkedIn automation. Used by agencies and growth teams globally.
Pricing: $69/mo (Starter), $159/mo (Pro), $439/mo (Team)
Strengths: 50+ LinkedIn automation workflows, cloud-based, runs 24/7, massive brand recognition
Weaknesses: Complex slot + hours + credits system; unused hours are lost monthly; bugs in email verification; users report "constant calculation game" to avoid hitting limits
Your edge: You offer comparable scraping with NO monthly limits, no slot restrictions, no expiring credits

2. Waalaxy (ex-ProspectIn)
Popular multi-channel tool with 150,000+ users and strong free tier.
Pricing: Free plan (200 invites/week), paid from ~$39/mo up to $490/mo for teams
Strengths: Beginner-friendly, Chrome extension, LinkedIn + email sequences, good brand trust
Weaknesses: Price hikes in 2025 drew criticism; recurring bugs in Email Finder; relies on Dropcontact for email enrichment (extra cost); no mobile app
Your edge: Your BYOK (bring your own Apify key) model means zero dependency on a third-party enrichment API

3. Dux-Soup
One of the oldest tools — good for beginners, popular with Pipedrive users.
Pricing: Free plan, paid from ~€12.99/mo; Turbo plan from €36.25/mo
Strengths: Simple, battle-tested, native Pipedrive integration, cheap entry point
Weaknesses: Requires Chrome browser open at all times; no cloud execution; not safe for large-scale scraping; slow interface; complex to learn
Your edge: Your app runs server-side via Apify — no browser needed, runs on schedule, fully automated

4. Evaboot
Niche tool focused specifically on exporting/cleaning Sales Navigator leads.
Pricing: From $9/mo
Strengths: Excellent data cleaning (removes emojis, typos), fast CSV exports from Sales Nav, loved by solopreneurs
Weaknesses: Requires Sales Navigator ($99.99/mo additional cost); very narrow use case — no engagement tracking, no post scrapers, no keyword tracking
Your edge: You cover both profile scraping AND post engagement extraction — Evaboot only does one thing

5. TexAu
No-code automation platform with LinkedIn and 100+ other platform workflows.
Pricing: From $29/mo (Desktop), $59/mo (Cloud)
Strengths: Multi-platform automation, no-proxy desktop mode, tech-savvy power users love it
Weaknesses: Recently disabled LinkedIn automation features; confusing interface for non-technical users; needs technical knowledge to configure
Your edge: Your app is purpose-built for LinkedIn — no setup confusion, focused UX

6. Linked Helper
Desktop application, very affordable, popular with budget-conscious users.
Pricing: From $15/mo
Strengths: Extremely affordable, human-click emulation for safety, works with all LinkedIn account types
Weaknesses: Desktop app — must keep computer on; no cloud scheduling; basic UI; no keyword or post engagement tracking
Your edge: Scheduled trackers + cloud Apify execution = no computer required


Category B — API / Developer Tools
Used by technical teams to build custom lead pipelines. Not direct user-facing competitors, but fight for the same budget.

7. Proxycurl
Developer API for structured LinkedIn data. The go-to for technical teams.
Pricing: From $49/mo (2500 credits) or pay-as-you-go from $10; $0.02 per profile
Strengths: Highest data accuracy (95%+), fresh real-time data, clean API, great developer docs
Weaknesses: Developer-only — zero UI; requires coding knowledge to use; no scheduling, no trackers, no built-in workflows
Your edge: You wrap Apify (similar infrastructure) with a full UI, trackers, and scheduling — same power, no code required

8. Apify (raw platform)
The infrastructure your app is built on. Also available as a standalone tool.
Pricing: Free tier + pay-as-you-go; $5/mo plans available
Strengths: Massive actor library, powerful, flexible, developer-friendly
Weaknesses: Requires significant technical knowledge to use; no UI for non-developers; users must configure each actor manually
Your edge: You are essentially Apify made easy — a polished UI layer on top of the same infrastructure


Category C — Multi-Channel Outreach Platforms
Tools focused on sending messages rather than scraping — they scrape as a byproduct, not a primary feature.

9. HeyReach
Agency-focused LinkedIn automation with multi-account management.
Pricing: $79/mo per seat; $799/mo for unlimited seats
Strengths: Multi-account rotation (bypass LinkedIn limits), unified inbox, strong CRM integrations, trusted by 1,100+ companies
Weaknesses: Very expensive for small teams; focused on outreach sequences, not data collection; no keyword tracking
Your edge: Data collection + enrichment is your core — HeyReach needs users to bring their own leads

10. La Growth Machine (LGM)
Premium multi-channel platform — LinkedIn + Email + Twitter outreach.
Pricing: From ~$60/mo
Strengths: 3.5x higher reply rates claimed; works without browser extension; Dropcontact built-in; AI personalization
Weaknesses: Premium price point; complex for beginners; no dedicated post engagement scraping or keyword tracking
Your edge: Keyword Tracker and Post Engagement Extractor are unique features not offered here

11. Lemlist
Cold email + LinkedIn hybrid — strong brand with AI personalization.
Pricing: From $59/mo
Strengths: AI-powered personalization, strong deliverability tools, multi-channel sequences
Weaknesses: Data scraping is secondary; focused on outreach not lead discovery; requires existing lead list
Your edge: You generate the lead list — lemlist sends to it. You are upstream in the funnel

12. Expandi
Cloud-based LinkedIn automation with safety as the primary selling point.
Pricing: From $99/mo
Strengths: Dedicated proxy per account, strong safety reputation, smart inbox
Weaknesses: Expensive; no data enrichment; no post engagement scraping; limited scraping capabilities
Your edge: Lower cost entry with comparable automation for data collection use cases


Category D — Database / Enrichment Platforms
Large B2B databases. They sell existing data rather than scraping fresh data. Compete for budget but serve a different use case.

13. Apollo.io
The most popular B2B database + outreach platform with 260M+ contacts.
Pricing: Free (50 exports/mo), paid from $49/mo
Strengths: Massive contact database, built-in email sequences, CRM integrations, generous free tier
Weaknesses: Data can be stale/inaccurate; 'very time-consuming on LinkedIn' to verify data per Reddit; users report frequent data quality issues
Your edge: You scrape FRESH, real-time LinkedIn data — Apollo sells cached data that may be months or years old

14. Lusha
Chrome extension database with 300M+ professional contacts.
Pricing: Free (5 credits/mo), paid from $36/user/mo
Strengths: Good browser integration, large database, phone number data
Weaknesses: Users report frequent data inaccuracies especially with emails and phone numbers; can be expensive for teams; no post engagement tracking

15. Clay
Data orchestration tool — pulls and enriches from 50+ sources including LinkedIn.
Pricing: From $149/mo
Strengths: Extremely powerful for technical users; AI enrichment; loved by sophisticated GTM teams
Weaknesses: Very expensive; steep learning curve; not beginner-friendly; requires integration setup
Your edge: Your app is purpose-built, easier to use, and dramatically cheaper for LinkedIn-focused use cases

16. Skrapp.io
Email extraction tool for LinkedIn with 2M+ professional users.
Pricing: Free (50 credits/mo), paid from ~$49/mo
Strengths: Fast (25 profiles/second), 200M+ prospect database, 92% email verification accuracy
Weaknesses: Email-focused only; no post engagement scraping; no keyword or profile tracker

17. Airscale
Free Sales Navigator scraper, repeatedly cited in Reddit as the go-to free option.
Pricing: Free (unlimited per Reddit users)
Strengths: Genuinely free; unlimited scraping from Sales Nav; beloved by budget users
Weaknesses: Very limited features — no engagement tracking, no keyword tracker, no profile enrichment beyond basic fields; no scheduling
Your edge: Broader feature set + scheduling + engagement extraction

18. Datablist
Safety-first Sales Navigator scraper — doesn't require your LinkedIn credentials.
Pricing: From $25/mo (500 leads/month at $0.05/lead)
Strengths: Account safety is #1 priority; doesn't need your cookies; 60+ enrichment tools
Weaknesses: Limited to Sales Navigator search scraping; no post engagement scraper; no keyword tracker

19. UpLead
B2B database with 108M+ LinkedIn contacts in 200+ countries.
Pricing: From $99/mo
Strengths: 50+ search filters, real-time email verification, CRM integrations
Weaknesses: Expensive; data freshness concerns; no LinkedIn post engagement tracking

20. Octopus CRM
Budget-friendly automation tool with built-in LinkedIn CRM.
Pricing: From $9.99/mo
Strengths: Extremely affordable, simple to use, built-in CRM for LinkedIn
Weaknesses: Chrome extension (browser must be open); limited scraping; no scheduling; no keyword tracking
